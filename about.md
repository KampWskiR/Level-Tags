# Level Tags

This mod allows you to see <cb>level's tags</cb> before playing the level, or <cy>search levels</cy> with or without specific tags.

**Tags are organised in the 4 categories:**

![-](frame:square_01_001.png?scale=0.35) **<co>Style tags</co>** lets you know if a level is a **standard level, design level, modern level** etc.

![-](frame:GJ_sMagicIcon_001.png?scale=0.75) **<cg>Theme tags</cg>** lets you know about the levels **themes** or **feeling**.

![-](frame:GJ_infoIcon_001.png?scale=0.45) **<cl>Meta tags</cl>** lets you know if the level is **impossible, a challenge, is epileptic, have a NONG, is a collab** etc.

![-](frame:gj_dartBtn_on_001.png?scale=0.35) **<cp>Gameplay tags</cp>** lets you know about the type of gameplay you will experience, **like bossfight, flow, memory, gimmicky** etc.
